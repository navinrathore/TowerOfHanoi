# Mission

MyTowerOfHanoi is a visual, educational platform designed to explore the classic Tower of Hanoi mathematical puzzle through different solving approaches. It bridges classic computer science algorithms with modern agentic AI by demonstrating recursive, search-based, and reinforcement learning solvers.

## What We Do

We provide:
1. **Interactive Gameplay**: A responsive web interface allowing users to play the Tower of Hanoi game, tracking their time, moves, and constraints (e.g. no larger disk on top of a smaller one).
2. **Classic Algorithmic Solvers**: Visual step-by-step execution of recursive and iterative solutions.
3. **Pathfinding Solvers**: Search-based solvers (such as A* search) showing the state-space and shortest path to completion.
4. **Reinforcement Learning Solver**: An interactive Q-learning agent that trains live (or loads pre-trained states) to solve the puzzle, showing its rewards and training statistics.
5. **Historical Analysis**: A dashboard for tracking game attempts and comparing move counts/efficiency between users, A*, and Q-learning solvers.

## Who We Serve

- **CS Students and Educators**: Who want to visualize recursion, state space search, and bitwise solvers.
- **AI/ML Hobbyists**: Who want to see a tangible, visual example of a Reinforcement Learning agent training in real-time.
- **Casual Puzzle Players**: Who want to test their speed and accuracy on the classic puzzle.

## Target Audience

- **Developers & Learners**: Seeking a reference implementation of FastAPI + Tailwind + SQLite + Reinforcement Learning.
- **Demonstrators**: Looking for a visually engaging, self-contained AI-centric demo.

## What Success Looks Like

A polished, fast-loading, and responsive web application where any user can start a game, trigger various solver algorithms, watch a Q-learning agent solve the game, and see clear statistical comparisons.
